Amenities

As a haven for tranquillity-seeking urbanites, Stil 1827 houses a slew of serenity-inducing amenities. From the wellness facilities to the leafy garden and the zen-like loggia, our property is an oasis of calm.

Aromatic flower beds. Tall leafy trees. Chirping native birds. Our garden sets the scene for a quaint country escape to savour serene silence amid breathtaking natural landscapes.

Spanning 800m2, the garden wraps around the property’s back, front, and sides. Expert landscape architect Peter Duerinck designed this green enclave to complement the historic property and lush surrounding region.

Originally a vegetable garden, the space has been transformed into a relaxing sanctuary for guests to disconnect in a spectacular setting. Lush grasses, native flowers and verdant shrubbery scatter around the grounds. Weathering steel garden borders encircle the perimeter for a rustic, industrial touch.

These sprawling grounds host a multitude of tranquil alcoves, from the high terrace to the wellness patio and the open-air barbeque. Secluded spaces provide a private open-air retreat, while shared areas evoke a more communal experience.

Direct access to a verdant, cow-peppered pasture provides a closer connection to nature and rural Belgian life. Wander over to the  “cow terrace,” a lofty patio for gazing at the herd below.
